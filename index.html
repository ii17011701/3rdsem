APPLICATION GUIDELINES FOR STUDENTS
-  Re-valuation (RV) / Re-totalling (RT) / Photo copy (PC) can be applied only through Online Portal within two 10 days from the date of result announcement.

-  Students will be shown the list of subjects in which he / she had appeared for the examination for which RV / RT / PC is intended.

-  On selecting the subjects, the relevant fee is automatically displayed.

-  On selecting required subjects, student need to select the mode of payment.

-  In case of Post Office Payment, Application along with challan will be printed. Keep the application and submit the challan at nearest post office and get the receipt for making the payment.

-  In case of Credit / Debit / Net Banking, Student needs to create an email id by clicking on "Create Account"

-  Enter a valid email id and mobile number. Select " I agree to PayUMoney Terms & Conditions " and Click on "Quick Sign up"

-  Now select Credit Card or Debit Card or Net Banking for making the payment.

-  After Successful payment, application will be printed. Keep the application form printed for future reference.

-  Unique application number is printed for each registration. Student has to use this application number for all correspondence with college / university.

-  Students no need to submit the application form at college.
